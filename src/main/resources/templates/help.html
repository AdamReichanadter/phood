Creating a recipe: create recipe page

*

If you find a recipe that you'd like to store to others, locate the nav bar. It should be in the upper position of your page. In that nav-bar click on the button named "Create Recipe"

*

You will need to insert a recipe name that suits that of your dish. Please keep in mind that this page will be made public so restrain from any profane words or dishes. Once you have inserted a name, go ahead an add a description that gives users and idea what your recipe will be such as tastes, and what events its good for.

*

Once you've created your description select the category for which your recipe falls into, then Go step by step the directions to cook your meal. For other people convenience

*

Once you've created a recipe add in all of the ingredient that will be required for your dish. The ingredient are listed in 3 columns, quantity, measurements, and description of what that ingredient is.

Example:

Quantity Unit Description
1 cup milk
